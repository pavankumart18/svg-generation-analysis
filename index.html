SVG Generation Benchmark

Evaluating AI models on their ability to generate SVG images from text prompts

This benchmark evaluates 10 AI models across 30 creative prompts, scored by an ensemble of vision models on 5 criteria.

Note: All models listed generate SVG directly. Vision models are used only as judges and are not shown in results.
Missing scores (-) indicate model output unavailable or excluded from aggregation.
